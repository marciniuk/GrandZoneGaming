Welcome to the official GrandZoneGaming website.
We are one of the largest Polish organizations of mobile players. Our activity is focused on Supercella games (Clash Royale, Clash of Clans, Brawl Stars), but we are also open to other games such as Call of Duty Mobile.
We actively participate in esports fights, organizing many different cyclical tournaments at Clash Royale. We are the organizer of the Christmas Charity Tournament, which in the first edition in 2019 was a great success, during which we collected over 32,000 PLN from donors, which we donated to the Warsaw Hospice for Children Foundation. The next edition of this event on December 22, 2020.
At the moment, we spend most of our energy on the GZG International Cup tournament. This tournament, as the name suggests, is for teams from all over the world - 29 invited internationals and 3 that will win the qualifiers: one of the international qualifiers (for 16 teams that did not receive a direct invitation) and two Polish teams that will take the first 2 places in local qualifications. We want to make this action a cyclical tournament, expanding it with stronger and stronger teams.
In the future, we want to create a league for the top 16 teams, which will play against each other after passing regional qualifiers for 64 teams.
Our organization often cooperates with well-known creators of Polish YouTube, we often organize various competitions and events in which you can win interesting prizes. We create our tournaments for all players - those from the top shelf, but also those less known. Our priority is to allow as many people as possible to appear in our competitions, which will allow us to create more world-class players in our Polish Supercell yard. This and much more to make the Polish mobile games community more enjoyable. We are sure it is worth following our website, telegram, twitter and fan page .
