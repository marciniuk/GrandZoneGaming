International league for players!
  1. League for 48 invited players and 16 players, who will qualify.
  2. In the 1st stage of elimination players will qualify when reached 1st-8th places in one of 4 tournaments in the game. Anyone who follows GZG on Twitter will be able to participate in the qualifying tournament.
  3. Everyone who will manage to join the tournament can take part in the eliminations. The full name of tournament and the password will be shared 1 hour before the tournament starts.
  4. Everyone who will finish on 1st-8th places will get to the 2nd stage of elimination where everyone will be assigned to the pairs according to the places in the tournament.
    • 1st player in tournament #1 will play with 8th player in tournament #3
    • 1st player in tournament #2 will play with 8th player in tournament #4
    • 1st player in tournament #3 will play with 8th player in tournament #1
    • 1st player in tournament #4 will play with 8th player in tournament #2
    • 2nd player in tournament #1 will play with 7th player in tournament #3
    • 2nd player in tournament #2 will play with 7th player in tournament #4
    • 2nd player in tournament #3 will play with 7th player in tournament #1
    • 2nd player in tournament #4 will play with 7th player in tournament #2
    • 3rd player in tournament #1 will play with 6th player in tournament #3
    • 3rd player in tournament #2 will play with 6th player in tournament #4
    • 3rd player in tournament #3 will play with 6th player in tournament #1
    • 3rd player in tournament #4 will play with 6th player in tournament #2
    • 4th player in tournament #1 will play with 5th player in tournament #3
    • 4th player in tournament #2 will play with 5th player in tournament #4
    • 4th player in tournament #3 will play with 5th player in tournament #1
    • 4th player in tournament #4 will play with 5th player in tournament #2
  5. Every matches will be played in BO5 with double-sided ban.
  6. The winners of these matches will join to the bunch of invited pleyers and will play GZG Players League.
  7. DOUBLE ELIMINATION BRACKET - We"ll draw all players to the main bracket with loser bracket. Players who lose their BO going to the loser bracket.
  8. Format in the main bracket:
    • 1/32 - BO3 with bans
    • 1/16 - BO3 with bans
    • 1/8 - BO5 with bans
    • 1/4 - BO5 with bans
    • 1/2 - BO5 with bans
    • Main bracket final
  9. Format in the loser bracket
    • 1/16 - BO3 with bans
    • 1/8 - BO3 with bans
    • 1/4 - BO5 with bans
    • 1/2 - BO5 with bans
    • Loser bracket final
  10. The losers from the previous brackets will fight for 3rd place, while the winners will meet in the grand final.
  11. Prizes:
    • 1st place - 900$
    • 2nd place - 400$
    • 3rd place - 200$
