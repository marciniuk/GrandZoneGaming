International league for players!
  1. League for 48 invited players and 16 players, who will qualify.
  2. In the 1st stage of elimination players will qualify when reached 1st-8th places in one of 4 tournaments in the game. The tournaments will be created for as many players as the quantity of likes on a pinned post on our Twitter GZG concerning this league.
  3. Everyone who will manage to join the tournament can take part in the eliminations. The full name of tournament and the password will be shared 1 hour before the tournament starts (Password will be changed after the tournament exceeds exactly the same number of players as the quantity of likes on pinned post).
  4. Players who will reached promoted entering to the league will be checked if they did simple actions from the pinned post on our Twitter before the league starts.
  5. Everyone who will finish on 1st-8th places and correctly did these tasks will get to the 2nd stage of elimination where everyone will be assigned to the pairs according to the places in the tournament.
    • 1st player in tournament #1 will play with 8th player in tournament #3
    • 1st player in tournament #2 will play with 8th player in tournament #4
    • 1st player in tournament #3 will play with 8th player in tournament #1
    • 1st player in tournament #4 will play with 8th player in tournament #2
    • 2nd player in tournament #1 will play with 7th player in tournament #3
    • 2nd player in tournament #2 will play with 7th player in tournament #4
    • 2nd player in tournament #3 will play with 7th player in tournament #1
    • 2nd player in tournament #4 will play with 7th player in tournament #2
    • 3rd player in tournament #1 will play with 6th player in tournament #3
    • 3rd player in tournament #2 will play with 6th player in tournament #4
    • 3rd player in tournament #3 will play with 6th player in tournament #1
    • 3rd player in tournament #4 will play with 6th player in tournament #2
    • 4th player in tournament #1 will play with 5th player in tournament #3
    • 4th player in tournament #2 will play with 5th player in tournament #4
    • 4th player in tournament #3 will play with 5th player in tournament #1
    • 4th player in tournament #4 will play with 5th player in tournament #2
  6. Every matches will be played in BO5 with double-sided ban.
  7. The winners of these matches will join to the bunch of invited pleyers and will play GZG Players League.
  8. DOUBLE ELIMINATION BRACKET - We"ll draw all players to the main bracket with loser bracket. Players who lose their BO going to the loser bracket.
  9. Format in the main bracket:
    • 1/32 - BO3 with bans
    • 1/16 - BO3 with bans
    • 1/8 - BO5 with bans
    • 1/4 - BO5 with bans
  10. Format in the loser bracket
    • 1/16 - BO3 with bans
    • 1/8 - BO3 with bans
    • 1/4 - BO5 with bans
  11. Top 4 players in the main and loser bracket going to the bracket where each player will play against each player BO5 with bans, for winning BO5 player get 3 pts and for lose 0 pts.
  12. More points you have, the higher you are in the table. If both players gain the same amount of points, we'll count the difference of amount of matches won to amount of matches lost. If this solution won't be solving we'll count the difference of amount of crowns gained to amount of crowns lost. If these solutions still won't be solving then the match between the both players which had played will be decisive.
  13. Prizes:
    • 1st place - 900$
    • 2nd place - 400$
    • 3rd place - 200$
